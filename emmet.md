> Emmet is a plugin for many popular text editors which greatly improves HTML & CSS workflow.  

Custom attributes

`script` expands to  
`<script></script>`

`script[src]` expands to  
`<script src=""></script>`

 `div#footer.class1.class2.class3` expands to
 `<div id="footer" class="class1 class2 class3">`
